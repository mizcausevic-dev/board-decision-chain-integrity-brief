# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Decision Chain Integrity Brief is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- convert handoff sprawl, chain breaks, and escalation gaps into explicit board-visible decision-path tradeoffs
- expose where locking a final decision path can recover decision speed before another lane is funded
- tie chain coverage and board confidence to named operating surfaces instead of abstract governance complaints
- convert scattered chain-integrity bottlenecks into one reusable intervention packet for boards and investors
